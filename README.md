# Creator Growth Suite

Discover opportunities. Create better content. Earn more clicks.

A collection of Claude skills designed to help creators identify content opportunities, develop stronger content strategies, and improve YouTube packaging performance.

## Included Skills

### Content Opportunities

Identify high-potential content opportunities based on audience demand, content gaps, underserved topics, content angles, and long-term growth potential.

Key capabilities:

- Content opportunity discovery
- Content gap analysis
- Audience demand analysis
- Content angle generation
- Content series development
- Opportunity prioritization
- Platform-aware recommendations

---

### Content Strategist

Transform content opportunities into actionable content plans.

Key capabilities:

- Content strategy development
- Audience analysis
- Hook generation
- Script development
- Content positioning
- Platform-specific optimization
- Engagement strategy
- CTA generation

---

### Thumbnail Strategist

Optimize YouTube packaging for stronger click-through rates and viewer interest.

Key capabilities:

- Thumbnail analysis
- Title analysis
- Thumbnail-title synergy analysis
- CTR optimization
- Audience psychology
- Competitive positioning analysis
- Packaging recommendations
- Alternative title and thumbnail concepts

---

## Workflow

Recommended workflow:

```text
Content Opportunities
        ↓
Content Strategist
        ↓
YouTube Thumbnail Strategist
```

1. Discover what content to create.
2. Develop the content strategy.
3. Optimize packaging for clicks.
